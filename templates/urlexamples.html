URL examples

frost v1 URL examples

To add more parameters to your URL request, see the list of parameter definitions.


  • Get the latest data for the Blindern station:
    • https://frost-rc.met.no/api/v1/obs/ranked/get?incobs=true&time=latest&elementids=air_temperature&stationids=18700
  • Get the metadata (without observations) for the 5 stations within 5km of Oslo:
    • https://frost-rc.met.no/api/v1/obs/ranked/get?incobs=false&nearest={"maxdist":5,"maxcount":5,"points":[{"lon":10.75,"lat":59.91}]}
  • Get a list of stations (metadata, without observations) in the county of Vestland measuring wind between 2021-01-01 00:00:00 UTC and 2022-01-01 00:00:00 UTC:
    • https://frost-rc.met.no/api/v1/obs/ranked/get?incobs=false&time=2021-01-01T00:00:00Z%2F2022-01-01T00:00:00Z&elementids=wind%2A&stationcounties=vestland
  • Get all the wind measurements (using * as wildcard) made in the municipality of Oslo in the time interval from 2022-12-19 00:00:00 UTC to 2022-12-19 03:00:00 UTC:
    • https://frost-rc.met.no/api/v1/obs/ranked/get?incobs=true&time=2022-12-19T00:00:00Z%2F2022-12-19T03:00:00Z&elementids=wind*&stationmunicipalities=oslo
  • Get a list of stations (metadata, without observations) in the county of Vestland measuring air temperature at 10m above the ground between 2021-01-01 00:00:00 UTC and 2022-01-01 00:00:00 UTC:
    • https://frost-rc.met.no/api/v1/obs/ranked/get?incobs=false&time=2021-01-01T00:00:00Z%2F2022-01-01T00:00:00Z&elementids=air_temp%2A&levels=10&stationcounties=vestland
